Please Note: PortaPayments will cease operating on the 30th of January 2013.

PortaPayments is a "Scan & Pay" solution which allows customers to make payments and purchase goods by scanning a QR code with their 'phone using any third party scanner such as i-nigma reader for iPhone and Barcode Scanner for Android.

Enter the recipient and amount below, click Generate Code, then use the generated QR code wherever you want people to make a payment (websites, printed media, shop windows, etc.).
